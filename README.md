## Hi everyone! 👋

- 🔭 I am a statistical astronomer interested in making new models of the many fascinating aspects of the joint assembly of galaxies and dark matter by pushing our datasets to be bigger, span more of cosmic time, and by innovating new advanced statistical and machine learning methods to analyse them.
- :spider_web: Within machine learning, I especially care about Geometric ML, and going beyond our simulations and actually doing inference on data --- an underdeveloped aspect of modern ML in astronomy.
- 📃 My publications can most reliably be found on [ADS](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/search/fq=%7B!type%3Daqp%20v%3D%24fq_database%7D&fq_database=(database%3Aastronomy%20OR%20database%3Aphysics)&p_=0&q=((author%3A%22Jespersen%2C%20Christian%20K.%22%20or%20author%3A%22Kragh%20Jespersen%2C%20Christian%22)%20AND%20year%3A2020-)&sort=date%20desc%2C%20bibcode%20desc).
